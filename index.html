About us

NuTech, Inc. pharmacists ensure that every radiopharmaceutical dose that is ordered by your facility is the right dose for the right patient at the right time - every time! NuTech, Inc. is confident of this level of service because every part of every prescription transaction is overseen by a registered pharmacist. Answering the phone, processing the prescription, preparing the radiopharmaceutical kit are all performed by pharmacists. This level of service and commitment to you, our customer, is unparalleled in the nuclear pharmacy market today.

Joint Commission Accreditation




NuTech, Inc. pharmacies have earned The Joint Commission Gold Seal of Approval for Accreditation by demonstrating compliance with the commission's national standard for healthcare quality and safety.

NuTech Inc.'s pharmacies are accredited under The Joint Commission's Home Care Accreditation Program.

Concerns about patient safety or quality of care provided by NuTech, Inc. may be addressed to Danny R. Allen, RPh, President at 903-592-8635 or email. Concerns may also be directed to The Joint Commission by submitting a fax to 630-792-5636 or completing an online form by accessing The Joint Commission website at www.jointcommission.org and choosing the Contact Us tab.

NNSA Recognition Awards




NuTech, Inc. was awarded with Recognition Award by NNSA DOE in recognition to provide radiopharmaceuticals produced with LEU since January 2018.

This effort is consistent with the Department of Energy/National Nuclear Security Administration's effforts to accelerate the establishment of reliable supplies of Mo-99 produced without highly enriched uranium.

NuTech, Inc. pharmacies have earned Recognition Award from United States Department of Energy for providing supplies of molybdenum-99/technetium-99m radiopharmaceutical products produced with low enriched uranium since January 2018.

This effort is consistent with the Department of Energy/National Nuclear Security Administration's effforts to accelerate the establishment of reliable supplies of Mo-99 produced without highly enriched uranium.
