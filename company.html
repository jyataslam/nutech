Learn About NuTech, Inc.

NuTech, Inc. is an independently owned, commercial nuclear pharmacy corporation operating class B (nuclear) pharmacies in Tyler, College Station and Wichita Falls, Texas. From these three state of the art facilities, NuTech, Inc. serves most of East and North Central Texas and Southern Oklahoma. NuTech, Inc. corporate headquarters are located in beautiful Tyler, Texas, "America's Rose Capital".

Our Company

NuTech, Inc. is a service-oriented healthcare business centered on offering 24 hour, 365 day a year preparation and delivery of unit-dose radioactive drugs for use in hospitals and freestanding clinics. Products and services provided include diagnostic radiopharmaceuticals, therapeutic radiopharmaceuticals, and non-radioactive and interventional agents used in conjunction with radiopharmaceuticals. The Company also provides radioactive sources used for calibration and quality control of nuclear imaging equipment, related nuclear medicine equipment and supplies. Physician continuing education programs, D.O.T. HAZMAT training, technical research and reporting, and RAM license consulting services are also available. NuTech, Inc. is staffed by fourteen nuclear pharmacists, most of whom are Board Certified, and a support staff consisting of Certified Pharmacy Technicians, office personnel, couriers, and quality control technicians.

Our Mission

The mission of NuTech, Inc. is to provide the highest quality nuclear pharmacy services defined as and evaluated by total customer satisfaction. This is achieved by employing motivated, well-trained employees working in a safe, professional, state-of-the-art environment that encourages and acknowledges excellence and pride in individual job and company performance. In return, NuTech, Inc. will provide a comfortable work environment that respects the individual, fosters teamwork, encourages initiative and creative thinking, and acknowledges superior performance of job duties.

A Brief History

Danny Allen founded NuTech, Inc. in 1989. Danny, a 1980 graduate of the University of Houston College of Pharmacy, recognized an opportunity to fill a vital need in the East Texas healthcare marketplace while working for a national nuclear pharmacy chain in the Dallas/Fort Worth Metroplex area. With commitments from a few hospitals in the Tyler/Longview area, he and his wife, Georganne, opened the business in a small, leased space in an office building, and delivered the first radiopharmaceutical doses in August of 1989. Since then, the company has grown steadily, adding additional pharmacists and support personnel, and moving into a new, state-of-the-art building in 1993.
NuTech Inc.'s service area expanded in April of 1999 when Danny opened a new pharmacy in College Station, Texas, home of Texas A&M University. NuTech, Inc. expanded again in 2005 with the opening of our Wichita Falls, Texas pharmacy. From these three pharmacies, NuTech, Inc. serves most of East Texas, North Central Texas and Southern Oklahoma.
